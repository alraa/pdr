Emergency Help

We offer three types of Emergency Data Recovery solutions: Standard Emergency Data Recovery; First Response
Emergency Data Recovery; and RAID Emergency Data Recovery.

Standard Emergency Data
Recovery

Recommended for anyone in need of emergency/expedited data recovery. Diagnostic completed in 4
hours after your device arrives at our lab. An engineer will work around the clock until your data is
successfully recovered. A customer service representative will update you periodically on the status of
your case, providing you with peace of mind regarding your lost data.

First Response Emergency
Data Recovery

Recommended if the data lost is business related. With the First Response Emergency Data Recovery
plan, the recovery of your data will become our utmost priority. Diagnostic completed within 3 hours or
sooner after your storage device arrives at our lab. Our highly trained First Response engineers, who
are used to working under pressure, will work 24/7 until your data is successfully recovered. A customer
service representative will update you frequently on the status of your case.

RAID Emergency Data Recovery

Recommended if the data lost is business related. Like the First Response plan, the recovery of your
data will become our number one priority. Diagnostic completed within 3 hours or sooner after
your RAID configuration hard drives arrive at our lab. Our exceptionally skilled RAID engineers, who
specialize in RAID configuration data recovery, will be assigned to your case. They will work nonstop
until your data is successfully recovered. A customer service representative will update you
regularly on the status of your case.

We're here for you 24/7/365!

Even if you experience complications with your hard drive beyond our business hours, we offer and
conduct emergency data recovery services 24/7/365 until we can retrieve your data intact. Regardless of
which plan you choose, you will know what is wrong with your drive and the chances for successful
