Logical Hard Drive
Recovery

Logical failures of hard drives can be as a result of
corrupted data; viruses. Malware and other malicious
programs; errors in software or programs; lost
partitions or accidental formatting of partitions; and
inadvertent or even malicious deletion of data on the
hard drive.

Whatever the case may be, data that is inaccessible
through conventional means on a hard drive must be
recovered using tools and resources wielded by an
expert data recovery specialist.

Phisical Hard Drive Recovery

With a physical hard drive data recovery project, the actual storage device has been damaged in some
way: via direct or accumulated trauma; through tampering; botched repairs, or some other accidental or
deliberate cause. In cases like this the data is generally preserved on the drive, but accessing it can be
extremely difficult when the device is physically damaged.

This type of data recovery requires technical skills and state-of-the-art equipment in order to successfully
extract your valuable data.
